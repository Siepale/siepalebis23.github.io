The RISEN Framework for AI Prompting

The RISEN framework is a 5-step structured prompting technique designed to improve AI outputs by providing clear context, constraints, and objectives.

  • 🔹 R – Role: Define the AI’s role (e.g., "Act as a Senior Accountant").
  • 🔹 I – Input: Clearly state the data or context the AI should use.
  • 🔹 S – Steps: Specify the logical steps the AI should follow.
  • 🔹 E – Examples: Provide formatting preferences or specific examples.
  • 🔹 N – Nuance: Add tone (e.g., "Professional yet engaging") or special constraints.

Industry Applications

Accounting

"Act as a senior accountant. Summarize the financial report focusing on revenue, profit margins, and risks..."

Healthcare

"Act as a compliance officer. Draft a policy update ensuring alignment with HIPAA regulations..."
