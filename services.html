Creative Media Production

Leveraging years of experience in animated film coordination, puppet theater, and professional video editing to create visually compelling stories that connect African narratives with global audiences.

Animation & Storytelling

Founded in Burkina Faso in 2005, Afrik Denou develops creative productions that combine traditional African storytelling with modern animation technologies.

Our productions include stop-motion animation, puppet theater, 2D animation, visual storytelling, and educational media content designed for international audiences, festivals, broadcasters, and digital streaming platforms.

Through artistic innovation and cross-border collaboration, we create authentic content with strong cultural identity and global commercial potential.

Our Creative Services

Professional production services designed to support film development, digital storytelling, and international creative collaboration.

Video Editing & Post-Production

Professional editing services for animation, documentary projects, promotional campaigns, educational media, and branded visual content. We focus on storytelling structure, pacing, sound integration, and cinematic presentation.

Animation Project Coordination

Coordinating animation productions from concept development through production and distribution. Our workflow includes scheduling, creative supervision, artist collaboration, and production management aligned with international standards.

Digital Content Strategy

Strategic development of visual media designed for educational platforms, festivals, broadcasters, streaming markets, and international audiences. We help shape culturally authentic stories into scalable digital content.

Our Creative Approach

Afrik Denou believes that animation and visual storytelling can preserve culture, inspire communities, and create meaningful global connections.

By integrating traditional puppetry with modern production technologies, we develop innovative media experiences that celebrate African heritage while remaining accessible to international audiences.

Our productions are built through collaboration with artists, animators, designers, writers, and cultural institutions across multiple countries.
