Afrik Denou

A creative production company specializing in puppet theater and animated film development, founded in 2005 in Burkina Faso, West Africa.

Who We Are

Afrik Denou operates at the intersection of cultural storytelling, artistic innovation, and international collaboration. We develop and produce high-quality animated and puppet-based content from concept to distribution.

Through partnerships across multiple countries, Afrik Denou creates culturally authentic productions with international commercial potential.

Our Mission

To amplify African stories through puppetry and animation while nurturing creative talent and sustainable production systems.

Our Vision

To become a leading African creative production company recognized globally for innovation and storytelling excellence.

Our Story

Founded by Sie L. Pale in 2005, Afrik Denou has grown into a cross-border creative network of artists, designers, writers, and animators working across Africa and international markets.

By combining traditional puppetry with modern animation technology, the company creates innovative content suitable for festivals, educational platforms, broadcasters, and streaming platforms.

Leadership

Sie L. Pale

Founder & Creative Director

Leads creative direction, strategic partnerships, and long-term development of Afrik Denou projects across Africa and international markets.

Our Services

Co-Production Development

Full project development from concept through production and distribution.

Creative Network

Connecting international artists, animators, writers, and designers for cross-cultural collaboration.

Production Support

On-the-ground operational coordination and production support in Burkina Faso.

Partnerships & Investment

Afrik Denou collaborates with producers, cultural institutions, educational organizations, and investors interested in culturally driven content with international market potential.

We welcome co-productions, grant collaborations, and strategic investment opportunities.

Let’s Build Together

We welcome collaboration inquiries, co-production proposals, partnership opportunities, and investment discussions worldwide.

We typically respond within 48 hours.
